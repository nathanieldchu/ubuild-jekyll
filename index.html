building a more sustainable world

Inspired by the natural world and driven to protect it, we are setting out to change the food system for the better. We're a public benefit company, meaning that we baked our mission into our corporate DNA (we're also nerds, which is why we use words like DNA). We're not making an industrial money machine. We're making delicious cheese and working towards lasting prosperity for our human and ecological communities.
Doing the right thing shouldn't have to taste like broccoli (but for the record, we really like broccoli).
